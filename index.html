Mizuo Peck was born and raised in New York City. She is internationally known for her role as Sacajawea in all three of the 20th Century Fox Night at the Museum movies alongside Ben Stiller, Robin Williams, and Rami Malek.
Other Film credits include A Case of You opposite Justin Long and Evan Rachel Wood; Almost in Love with Alan Cumming; and Scenes of the Crime with Jeff Bridges.
Her Television credits include Madam Secretary starring Tea Leoni; Law & Order: Criminal Intent opposite Chris Noth and Annabella Sciorra; a recurring role on All My Children; and the supernatural WB drama, WitchBlade.
Mizuo has done commercials and print campaigns for many brands including Chevy, Levi's, Suave, Verizon, and Oil of Olay. Her image has graced gallery walls across the globe in the work of visual artist Barbara Probst. She has also been shot by acclaimed photographer Bruce Weber for Lâ€™Uomo Vogue.
Voice Over work includes commercials for KitchenAid and Glade Candles; narrating This is AI, a Discovery Channel documentary; as well as many audiobooks for Simon & Schuster. Her voice was also the soundtrack to an art installation at the Whitney Biennale.
In Theater, Mizuo has performed at the NY International Fringe Festival; the Samuel French Play Festival; and the New Performance Series at the Incubator Arts Project. At the Public Theater, she was in Suzan-Lori Park's 365 Days/365 Plays and All's Well That Ends Well as a company member of their exclusive Shakespeare Lab Program. Mizuo has a daughter and lives in lower Manhattan.
